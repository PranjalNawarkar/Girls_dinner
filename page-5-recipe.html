Dessert: S'Mores

I want more :
INGREDIENTS:
  • Graham crackers
  • Mini marshmallows
  • Chocolate chips

    •
    DIRECTIONS:

    Begin, by placing down some graham cracker halves on a microwave-safe plate. Then place some chocolate on top of the graham cracker. Follow that up with the marshmallow. If you don’t want the chocolate melted, leave it off.

    Now put the plate in the microwave and cook the S’mores on high for 20 to 30 seconds until the marshmallows puff way up about twice their size. Next remove the plate from the microwave, be careful, the plate may be hot!
