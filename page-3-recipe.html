Snack: Adult Lunchable

For when you want to eat with your fingers... like a grown up
INGREDIENTS:
  • Fancy Cheeses, like gouda, manchego, brie, etc.
  • Meats, like proscuitto, salami, smoked meats, and some people even like tinned fish like sardines!
  • Dips, like hummus or spichach artichoke dip
  • Fruit, like sliced apples or strawberries
  • Something Crunchy, like chips, pretzels, or nuts
  • Pickles...or some other slightly vinegary item to add a little sour flavor to the mix

    •
    DIRECTIONS:

    This is an easy one... Arrange all your ingredients on a serving platter and enjoy!
