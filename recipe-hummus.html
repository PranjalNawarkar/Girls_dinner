Appetizer: Hummus

Add garlic... to taste
INGREDIENTS:
  • 15 ounce can chickpeas, drained and rinsed
  • ⅓ cup tahini
  • 1½ tablespoons extra-virgin olive oil
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice, to taste
  • ¼ teaspoon garlic powder, to taste
  • ½ teaspoon sea salt
  • ¼ cup water, or as needed to blend
  • Veggies and/or warm pita bread, for serving

    •
    DIRECTIONS:

    In a blender, place the chickpeas, tahini, olive oil, lemon juice, garlic powder, and salt. Use the blender baton to blend until very smooth, adding water as needed to blend, and/or to reach your desired consistency.

    Transfer to a serving plate and serve with warm pita and veggies.
