Tres Leches

Three's a Party!
INGREDIENTS:
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 1/2 tsp baking powder
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 5 large eggs
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 12 oz evaporated milk
  • 14 oz sweetened condensed milk
  • 1 cup whole milk
  • 2 cups whipped cream
  • Ground cinnamon or fresh fruit for garnish (optional)

    •
    DIRECTIONS:

    Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C) and grease a 9x13-inch baking dish. Mix flour, baking powder, and salt in a bowl. Cream the butter and sugar, then add eggs and vanilla. Gradually add the dry mix to the wet mix and spread the batter in the baking dish.

    Bake for 30-35 minutes and let it cool slightly. Poke holes in the cake and pour the three milk mixture over it.

    Refrigerate for at least 2 hours. Top with whipped cream and garnish if desired.
