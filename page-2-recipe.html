Drink: Blended Sangria Margarita

Cheers to more fruit in your diet
SANGRIA INGREDIENTS:
  • Dry red wine
  • Frozen berries
  • Sweetener (I support Canada and their maple syrup)
  • Orange juice (if that’s yo thang, it sweetens nicely)
  • Triple sec (to your heart’s desire, I add the alcohol when I make the margarita!)
  • Sometimes ice, depending on your preferred crunch level in a slush

    • MARGARITA INGREDIENTS:
  • Tequila (as much to your heart’s content)
  • Lime juice (i like enough to make my lips pucker from the dry red and lime together)
  • Ice
  • Triple sec (remember how I talked about adding more alcohol before?)
  • People say agave, my first love is maple syrup, and thus that is what I add

    • DIRECTIONS:

    Now, no hate if you never make it to the second part of the recipe. I actually will make the margarita first, pour it in my glasses so the white slush is in the bottom half, then reuse the blender for the sangria to later pour on top of the glass. Pretty, aesthetic, tipsy…
