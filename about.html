The Girls of Girl Dinner

The best of Girl Dinner is that each girl gets to define it for herself.

Christie was born and raised in San Antonio and now hails from Arlington, Virginia. Her significant other does most of the cooking… but Christie is always happy to throw together Girl’s Dinner for her besties. She likes healthy living and is an avid boater and adventurer.
Ashe is a cat-owner who enjoys cooking as a hobby that provides edible food within thirty minutes. Her cat finds this very amusing. She enjoys reading recipes that aren’t cluttered with ads and creating her own culinary substitutions. On the days cooking fails - there is always coffee.
Pranjal is born and brought up in India, raised on her mom's delicious food. But now she's far from home, learning to cook and trying global desserts with her squad at Girl's Dinner every weekend. She loves to dance and likes having her skincare routine on point. Plus, she's all about that machine learning and advocates for women in ML field.
Sierra Marie Bonn is a local business advocate, non-profit founder ("Let’s Go Full STEAM Ahead!"), author, and proud Kansan. She is an enthusiastic foodie and avid traveler, always on the look-out for new restaurants to try and experiences to have. She collects antique & vintage cookbooks, one of her favorites is called "Standing Ovations," which was a fundraiser for a local symphony group in the 1970s.
Denae has been in Wichita her whole life and has loved all of the 1200+ restaurants in her hometown since she was little. When she isn’t café hopping—even though Cocoa Dolce reigns supreme in her heart—she’s at home, figuring out her next favorite meal. Denae is your classic homebody, a fan of charcuterie boards, books, board games, Prosecco, and hot tea.
